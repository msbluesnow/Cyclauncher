Cyclauncher is a modern, minimalist Android launcher built with Jetpack Compose.
It features a unique rectangular alphabet wheel for fast and intuitive app navigation.
The UI is optimized for one-handed use and supports dynamic accent colors.

Key Features:
- Unique rectangular alphabet wheel.
- One-handed optimization (Left/Right hand support).
- Dynamic accent colors and adaptive shadows.
- Fast, minimalist, and battery-efficient.
